## _My NEJM in the journal online_

Individual subscribers can store articles and searches using a feature on the *Journal*'s website (NEJM.org) called "My Account." Each article and search result links to this feature. Users can create personal folders and move articles into them for convenient retrieval later.

Disclosure forms provided by the authors are available with the full text of this article at NEJM.org.
